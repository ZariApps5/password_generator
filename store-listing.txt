Password Generator is the fastest way to create strong, secure passwords on your phone. No ads, no internet required, no accounts — just pure, offline security.

🔒 STRONG BY DEFAULT
Every password is generated with cryptographically random characters, guaranteeing true randomness. You control the length (4–64 characters) and which character types to include: uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and symbols.

⚡ INSTANT GENERATION
Tap once to generate. Tap again to copy. Your new password is on the clipboard in under a second.

📊 STRENGTH METER
A real-time strength indicator shows you exactly how secure your password is — Weak, Fair, Good, or Strong — based on length and character variety.

🕓 HISTORY
The last 8 generated passwords are saved in your session so you can always go back and copy a previous one.

🌙☀ DARK & LIGHT MODE
Choose the theme that suits you — a deep indigo dark mode for low-light environments, or a clean light mode for daytime use. Your preference is saved automatically.

✅ 100% OFFLINE
No internet connection required, ever. Your passwords never leave your device.

PERFECT FOR:
• Creating passwords for new accounts
• Replacing weak or reused passwords
• Security-conscious users who want full control

No tracking. No analytics. No permissions required.
